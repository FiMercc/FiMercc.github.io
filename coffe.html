MOCHA

Mocha is an espresso-based coffee. It consists of espresso and milk. It is a latte flavored with sugar and some syrups.

LATTE

Latte means milk in Italian. Its real name is Caffe Latte Machiato. Espresso is added to a mug filled with steam-heated viscous milk.

FILTER

Filter coffee is a type of coffee obtained as a result of brewing ground coffee beans with hot water. The brewed coffee is filtered
and ready to be served.
